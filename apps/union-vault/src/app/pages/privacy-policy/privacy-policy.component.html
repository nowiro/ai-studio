1. Data Controller

The data controller for UnionVault.eu is:

UnionVault.eu
Warsaw, Poland
E-mail: privacy@unionvault.eu

UnionVault.eu is a pan-European financial and real-estate information platform aggregating publicly available data from 27 EU countries. The service is informational only and does not constitute financial advice.

2. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

In accordance with Article 37 GDPR, UnionVault.eu has designated a Data Protection Officer. You may contact the DPO at:

Data Protection Officer — UnionVault.eu
E-mail: dpo@unionvault.eu
Postal address: UnionVault.eu, Attn: DPO, Warsaw, Poland

The DPO is independent, does not receive instructions regarding the exercise of tasks, and reports directly to the highest management level.

3. What Data We Collect

3.1 Data you provide voluntarily

  • Name and e-mail address when submitting the contact form.
  • Organisation name (optional).
  • Message content and selected contact topic.

3.2 Data collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate geolocation (country level) for access logging.
  • Browser type, operating system, and referring URL (standard HTTP headers).
  • Page views and navigation events (analytics, see Section 5).

3.3 Data stored locally on your device

  • Selected country preference ( uv_country ).
  • Selected language preference ( uv_language , uv_language_preferences ).
  • Cached exchange-rate data ( uv_fx_rates_v1 ) with a 1-hour TTL.

All locally stored data remains on your device. It is never transmitted to UnionVault.eu servers and is used solely to restore your preferences across sessions.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

5.1 Strictly necessary (no consent required)

Key Storage Purpose TTL
uv_country localStorage Remembers selected EU country Until cleared
uv_language localStorage Remembers selected UI language Until cleared
uv_language_preferences localStorage Per-country language overrides Until cleared
uv_fx_rates_v1 localStorage Cached exchange rates (reduces API calls) 1 hour

UnionVault.eu does not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or third-party social-media pixels.

5.2 How to manage cookies

You can clear localStorage data at any time via your browser settings ( DevTools → Application → Local Storage or equivalent). Clearing this data will reset your country and language preferences.

6. Retention Periods

  • Contact-form data: retained for up to 12 months after the last correspondence, then securely deleted.
  • Server access logs: retained for 90 days for security purposes.
  • Anonymised analytics data: retained for up to 24 months .
  • localStorage data: stored on your device until you clear it (no server copy).

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR you have the following rights:

Right of access (Art. 15)
You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
Right to rectification (Art. 16)
You may ask us to correct inaccurate data.
Right to erasure (Art. 17)
You may request deletion of your personal data (“right to be forgotten”).
Right to restriction (Art. 18)
You may request that we restrict processing in certain circumstances.
Right to data portability (Art. 20)
You may receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Right to object (Art. 21)
You may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3))
Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: privacy@unionvault.eu or the DPO at dpo@unionvault.eu . We will respond within 30 days (extendable to 60 days for complex requests per Art. 12(3) GDPR).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority. In Poland: UODO (uodo.gov.pl) . A full list of EU supervisory authorities is available at edpb.europa.eu .

9. International Transfers

UnionVault.eu processes and stores data within the European Economic Area (EEA). Where third-party services involve transfers outside the EEA (e.g. certain API providers), appropriate safeguards are in place under Chapter V GDPR (Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions).

10. Children's Privacy

UnionVault.eu is not directed at children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact privacy@unionvault.eu and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when the most recent revision was made. For material changes, we will place a prominent notice on the website homepage.
